guinea pig

A guinea pig is a small, furry animal that is often kept as a pet. It does not come from Guinea, and it is not a pig.

Guinea pigs are originally from South America. They are social animals and like to live with other guinea pigs. They eat hay, vegetables, and special guinea pig food.

Guinea pigs do not climb or jump much, so they need a flat, safe space to play. They make cute sounds like squeaks and purrs to show how they feel. With good care, a guinea pig can live for about 5 to 7 years.
